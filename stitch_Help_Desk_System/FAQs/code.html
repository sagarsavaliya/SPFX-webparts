How do I reset my domain password?

expand_more

To reset your domain password, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Self-Service Password Portal.
  2. Click on "Can't access your account?".
  3. Select "Work or School Account".
  4. Enter your user ID and the captcha characters.
  5. Follow the verification steps sent to your mobile device.
info Note: New passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include special characters.

How to request VPN access for remote work?

expand_more

VPN access requires manager approval. Please submit a New Ticket selecting category Network > VPN Access.

Once approved, you will receive an email with configuration instructions for the Cisco AnyConnect client.

Outlook is not syncing on my mobile device

expand_more

Often, re-adding your account resolves sync issues:

  • Open Outlook App settings.
  • Select your account and tap "Delete Account".
  • Restart the app and sign in again.

If the issue persists, ensure your device OS is up to date.

Can I install company software on my personal computer?

expand_more

No. For security and licensing compliance, company-licensed software may only be installed on company-issued assets. However, Microsoft 365 (Office) allows you to log in on up to 5 devices using your work credentials for web access.
